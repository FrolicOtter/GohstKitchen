How to Play

Your job is to fill the order at the top of the screen. Put the exact requested food on the special table before too much time passes. Faster orders pay more coins.

Core Controls

- Move with the arrow keys or the default UI movement keys.
- Press Q and E to move through fridge items or storage slots.
- Press W to use the selected spot: take food, place food, pick food back up, serve, or trash.
- Click an item in the fridge or a storage inventory to select that slot and use it immediately.
- Press Esc to pause. Resume keeps your saved coin count loaded.

Inventory

- You only have one held item slot.
- If your hand is empty, W usually picks something up.
- If you are holding food, W usually places it where you are facing.
- Spoiled food cannot be stored. Throw it away at the trash can.

Making Orders

1. Check the order icons at the top of the screen.
2. Take food from the fridge. The fridge starts with one of each item and restocks over time.
3. Use counters as temporary space when your one slot is full.
4. Use cutting boards for cut orders.
5. Use the oven for cooked orders.
6. Put the finished items on the special table.

Reading Food Icons

Order icons can ask for plain, cut, or cooked food. Match both the food and the status:

- Plain means the normal item.
- Cut means it must be prepared on a cutting board.
- Cooked means it must be prepared in the oven.

Special Table

The order completes only when the special table has exactly the requested items. If the table is full but wrong, a red X appears above it. Pick items back up, fix the mistake, and try again.

Tips

- Use Q and E when you want precision.
- Use clicking when you already know the slot you want.
- Keep food moving. Items left out too long spoil.
- DLC can add more food and content from the DLC menu before loading the game.
