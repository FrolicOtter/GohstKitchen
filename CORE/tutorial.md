How to Play

Fill the order shown at the top of the screen. You earn coins when the special table has exactly the requested items, and faster orders pay more.

Controls:

- Move with the arrow keys or the default UI movement keys.
- Press Q and E to cycle through fridge items or storage slots.
- Press W to grab, place, pick up, serve, or trash an item.

Kitchen flow:

1. Stand by the fridge, use Q and E to choose an item, then press W to take it.
2. Use counters, cutting boards, the oven, and storage spots to prepare food.
3. Put the three requested items on the special table.
4. When the special table matches the order, the order completes and coins are awarded.

Order icons can ask for plain, cut, cooked, or cooked meat items. Match both the food and its status before placing it on the special table.

Tips:

- Food left out too long spoils and cannot be placed in storage.
- If the special table is full but the order is wrong, a red X appears above it.
- Use the trash can with W to clear a held item you no longer need.
- DLC can add more content; install packs from the DLC menu before loading the game.
